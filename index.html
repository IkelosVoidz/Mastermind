Master Mind

Game Explanation

This is an online version of the popular game MasterMind, originally played by two players: a codemaker and a codebreaker, where the codemaker thinks of a random 4-digit number and the codebreaker tries to guess it. The codebreaker has 10 rounds to try to guess the code and at each round he tries to guess the number and the codemaker gives him feedback of how close he is from actually finding the answer. At every round, the codemaker gives 2 types of feedback: how many digits the other player guessed in the right position and how many are guessed right but in the wrong position. In this version of it, the computer will be the codemaker and you will be the codebreaker.
